Site Name

Great Sky HOA

Great Sky HOA is a website for the Homeowners Association (HOA) for the Great Sky Neighborhood in Canton GA.

Site Purpose

The purpose of the site is so that residents of the neighborhood can get info, announcements, and see upcoming events. This will foster community engagement.

Scenarios

Where can I find relevant information and community announcements?

What are the upcoming events going on in the neighborhood?
