Great Sky HOA

About

Great Sky is found deep in the woods at the foothills of the Blue Ridge mountains in northern Georgia. A small community of homes surrounded by quiet nature. Find your forever home or getaway house in the mountains. Great Sky offers only the best in amenities and activities for the whole family. Come discover a serene mountain retreat where adventure and relaxation meet.

Location

Located in Canton, GA at the foothills of the Blue Ridge Mountains, Great Sky offers more than just a great view of the sky. Bordering the beautiful Hickory Log Creek Reservoir, water activities are a plenty throughout the neighborhood. Well maintained nature hiking trails are all through the nearby mountains.

In addition to its natural beauty, Great Sky is conveniently located near a variety of shopping, dining, and entertainment options in Canton. The charming downtown area offers local boutiques, restaurants, and cultural attractions, providing a perfect blend of small-town charm and modern conveniences.

Amenities

Great Sky boasts a wide range of amenities designed to enhance your lifestyle and provide endless entertainment options. Enjoy our state-of-the-art clubhouse, complete with a fitness center, swimming pools, and a relaxing spa. For outdoor enthusiasts, we offer tennis courts, basketball courts, and a playground for children.

Take advantage of our beautifully landscaped parks and picnic areas, perfect for family gatherings or a peaceful afternoon. With an extensive network of hiking and biking trails, as well as easy access to the Hickory Log Creek Reservoir for fishing, boating, and kayaking, there's always something exciting to do at Great Sky.
